## Key Takeaways

### The Meta Path (Deadeye KB)
- **Deadeye** + **Kinetic Blast** + **Maloney's Mechanism** quiver
- Bow attack auto-triggers curse spells, doom explosion fires at 1s cooldown
- Forbidden Flesh + Flame (48%) to steal an extra ascendancy node (often Inevitable Judgement from Inquisitor)
- No manual curse casting required — fully automated

### The Bouyou Path (Inquisitor Caster)
- **Inquisitor** with native **Inevitable Judgement** + **Righteous Providence**
- Manual Conductivity casting with Spell Echo — doom caps faster, higher burst potential
- Rathpith Globe + Ghostwrithe = life-based ES hybrid, high block
- Pain Attunement (+30% more spell damage on low life) is a strong exclusive multiplier
- Strongest at single-target boss kills where crit procs Inevitable Judgement consistently

### Upgrade Priority for bouyou
1. **Watcher's Eye** (53% of meta) — look for `Damage Penetrates X% Lightning Resistance while affected by Conductivity` or similar mods
2. **Forbidden Flesh + Flame** (48%) — steal Inevitable Judgement if running a different ascendancy, or steal Chieftain/Slayer nodes for more damage
3. **Thread of Hope** (33%) — enables grabbing distant notable clusters without pathing cost
